Dead End on Netflix is an animated version of Hamish Steele's DeadEndia series of
graphic novels, but the first season of the new program also serves as a brilliant
illustration of the wonder LGBTQ creators can do when given the tools and freedom
to create their own tales.

Although the story of wayward youths who find themselves after being drawn into the
paranormal activities of a haunted theme park in Dead End is uniquely its own, the
fact that it even exists is a tribute to the influence other recent progressive-minded
cartoons have had on contemporary animation.

The show came out on Netflix on the 16th of June, 2022.
The second season of the show came out on Netflix on the 13th of October, 2022.
Netflix's Dead End probably wouldn't exist or be able to be the big-hearted, emotionally
sophisticated, all-ages fun that it is without series like Steven Universe, She-Re and
the Princesses of Power, and The Owl House.
